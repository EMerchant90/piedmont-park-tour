Doggie Dash 5k Run!

Description: The Piedmont Park Conservancy presents the 5th Year Anniversary of the Doggie Dash! This year, we are hosting a shorter “Dash” in addition to our 5K! This dog friendly event invites participants with and without dogs to experience the Park alongside four-legged friends to benefit the Piedmont Park Dog Parks. Flapping ears and wagging tails are encouraged. After the dash and 5K, hand out in the sponsor circle for cool swag and information from our dog vendors. All proceeds will be used to maintain and enhance the Piedmont Park Dog Parks.

Timings: March 11 @ 9:00 am - 11:00 am, 8:00 AM – Registration Opens

Location: The walk/run begins at the Promenade at Piedmont Park.

Pick up and Pitch

A lot goes into maintaining and enhancing Atlanta’s favorite urban green space! With more than 4 million visitors annually, we need your help to keep Piedmont Park beautiful. Pitch in by picking up litter in the park. Grab some gloves, a trash bag, and trash grabber; and head out into the park to keep the park clean and green year-round. Every stray straw, plastic bottle, bottle cap, and chip bag collected makes a difference!
Tuesdays and Thursdays from 9am – 11am
Project takes place outdoors
This opportunity is self-guided
Age requirement: 5 and up. Volunteers under the age of 18 must have a parent, legal guardian, or adult chaperone volunteer with them
All volunteers must sign a liability waiver
Volunteers under the age of 18 must have this waiver signed in advance by their parent or legal guardian
